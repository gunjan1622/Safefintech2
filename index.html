ABOUT NFLQ

The Financial Literacy Olympiad 2025 is a national-level competition hosted by Sodhani Academy of Fintech Enablers Limited to promote financial awareness and empower students to combat cyber fraud, scams, and money mismanagement.


Students will explore the world of budgeting, investing, saving, insurance, and entrepreneurship — all while competing in a fun and rewarding environment.

WHY PARTICIPATE

  • Develop essential money management skills
  • Boost decision-making and financial awareness
  • Learn to protect yourself from cyber frauds and scams
  • Win exciting prizes and recognition
  • Receive a Certificate of Participation & Excellence
  • Get 50% scholarship on a selected course from SAFE Academy (Scholarship up to ₹1 Crore)

With over 500 schools on board, we are fostering financial literacy among students nationwide through the National Finance Olympiad
